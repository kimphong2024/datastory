Cross-Cutting Insights

AI augments, not replaces. Only 3% of orgs have fully embedded AI into insights. 94% say it enables faster decisions, but 52% cite data privacy as the top barrier. The future insight professional needs prompt engineering skills.

Inclusion drives growth. Progressive advertising delivers +3.5% short-term and +16.3% long-term sales lift. 60% of Cannes Grand Prix winners embedded inclusion. Only 23% of people globally feel well-represented by advertisers.

Evolution of Consumer Insights
2015 — 2026
2015-2016
Foundation era. Focus on brand tracking fundamentals, concept testing methodologies, and the "7 Deadly Sins" of marketing that frustrate consumers. The future of insights begins to be questioned.
2017
Digital transformation accelerates. Social listening emerges as critical. VR shows promise for accessing subconscious consumer responses. 11,000+ Europeans surveyed on data-driven advertising attitudes. Cultural insight gains recognition.
2018-2019
Measurement matures. KPI frameworks for omnichannel emerge. Research methodologies benchmarked. London Insight Forum shapes global agenda. APAC markets gain focus.
2020
Pandemic pivots everything. Customer experience reinvented through behavioral science. E-commerce penetration jumps from 16% to 33% in 3 months. The Economist predicts digital acceleration and corporate leadership expectations surge — 80% believe companies drive positive change.
2021-2022
Inclusive insights emerge. Consumer happiness linked to brand success. Chinese brand growth reshapes global markets. ESOMAR calls for inclusive research samples and accessible methodologies. DEI enters the insights agenda.
2023
Generative AI enters. WFA benchmarks ROI measurement, core competencies, and consumer intimacy. Generative AI positioned as a decision-support tool. Cultural understanding recognized as "messy but essential."
2024
Strategic transformation. New rules defined: insights teams must move from operational to business-critical. Kantar's Brand Inclusion Index launches across 18 countries. Five dramatic shifts identified in how insight functions operate. AI adoption surveyed: only 3% fully embedded.
2025-2026
Inclusive by design, strategic by necessity. WFA publishes practical framework for embedding inclusion across the research lifecycle. Insight Forum triples to 600+ leaders. Insights partnerships emerge: 52% view insights as strategic, but 48% still see it as tactical. 75% formalize training with storytelling as the "anchor skill." Career development and pipeline support become strategic imperatives.
